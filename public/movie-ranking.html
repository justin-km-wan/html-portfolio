The Best Anime According to Justin

My TOP 3 Animes of All Time

Naruto

It is an inspiration that a young ninja who has a dream to be the top, which is being a Hokage. He never gives up to level up himself to be stronger and also made a lot of friends along his journey. He managed to show the world he is capable to save his comrades through the final battle.

Attack on Titan

A young boy who had a vision to save his city, where he is not aware that he was the chosen one from the beginning until he realised he had the power. In the end, he chose to bear all the sins by himself by saving his friends and the world with his final resolution on destroying the human population.

Demon Slayer

A young boy who is motivated to find out the source of the chaos which the demons appear at night to kill humans. One of the reason is because his family was killed by demons, fortunately his sister didnt die but was transformed into a demon which she able to control herself later on. He wants to believe that there is a cure to heal his sister. However, the journey is not that simple.
